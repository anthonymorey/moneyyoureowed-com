Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Who we are

Money You're Owed ("we", "us") publishes educational content about US consumer finance and sells related digital products at moneyyoureowed.com. Contact: hello@moneyyoureowed.com.

What we collect

If you sign up for our free resources or newsletter, we collect the email address (and any name) you provide. If you buy one of our digital products, we collect the name and email you enter at checkout and the order details needed to deliver it; your card payment is processed by Stripe and we never see or store your full card number. We also collect basic, non-identifying analytics about how our pages are used.

How we use it

To send you the free resource you requested, our newsletter, and occasional related offers; to process, deliver, and support any purchase you make (including refunds); and to improve our site. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link; you can opt out at any time.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of trusted processors solely to run the service: MailerLite (stores our email list and sends our newsletters/marketing), Stripe (processes payments), Resend (sends transactional emails such as your download links and receipts), and Cloudflare (hosts the site, routes our email, and provides privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics). Each processor's own privacy terms apply to that processing. We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices and rights

You can unsubscribe anytime via any marketing email, or request access to or deletion of your data by emailing hello@moneyyoureowed.com. We honor applicable rights under US state privacy laws and the UK/EU GDPR.

Cookies

Our analytics are cookieless. Our pages may use minimal cookies only where strictly necessary (for example, to complete a secure checkout). You can control cookies in your browser.

Payments

We sell digital products (such as the Complete Recovery Kit). Payments are processed securely by Stripe; we do not store your card details. We use the information you provide at checkout to fulfil and support your order and to honor our refund guarantee.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site and in our emails are affiliate links. If you click one and sign up or buy, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This disclosure is made under the U.S. Federal Trade Commission's guidelines (16 CFR Part 255).

Children

Our content is for adults; we don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

Changes

We'll update this page and change the "last updated" date if our practices change.
